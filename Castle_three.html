Medzhybizh Castle is a castle-fortress , located in the village of Medzhibozh , in the upper reaches of the Southern Bug River, at the confluence of the Buzhok River. First mention of the city of Mezhybozh dates back to the end of the 11th century. Medzhybizh Castle received the status of an architectural monument in 1963.Since 2020, Medzhybizh Castle has been an associated member of the FORTE CULTURA European Cultural Route of Fortification Monuments. There is a museum on the territory of the castle
